Simple Workflow

Compliant from Day One. Every Time.

1

Landlord Verified

Upload landlord ID and right-to-rent documents. Mark profile as verified.

2

Property Compliant

Upload EPC, Gas Safety Certificate, EICR, and all required certificates.

3

Tenant Verified
